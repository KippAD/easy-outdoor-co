Frequently Asked Questions

Here you will find questions that we get asked a lot, we hope they will help you with any queries you might have regarding our store. For more information feel free to contact us and we'll get back to you as soon as we can!

At our store, we want to make sure that you are 100% satisfied with your purchase. That is why we offer a generous return policy for items purchased from us. Our return policy ensures that you can return any item within 30 days of purchase, provided it is in new condition and in its original packaging.

We will provide a full refund or exchange for all returns, excluding shipping costs. If you have any questions about our return policy, please feel free to contact us at any time.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at using our contact form. Please include your order number in the message. We will provide you with further instructions on how to complete the return process upon recieving your message.

Orders placed in the UK will typically be processed and delivered within 1-2 working days. However, order processing and delivery times for international orders will vary depending on the country of delivery. We recommend that customers allow for additional time when placing orders to destinations outside of the UK.

We accept Visa and Mastercard for all our payments. All transactions are securely processed using Stripe, the leading payment processor. With Stripe, you can be sure that all your payments are secure and safe.

There is no minimum order requirement for our products and services. However, there is a delivery fee associated with each order that must be paid in order to receive the product or service. This fee pertains to 5% of the total cost of the items unless the order is above our £30 delivery threshold.

At Easy Outdoor we understand that saving money is as important as ever right now. That's why we offer a variety of sales and promotions throughout the year. You can view discount products in our Sale tab at the top of the page.

Our newsletter also keeps you up to date with all of our current promotions, so make sure to subscribe to stay in the know. With our sales and promotions, you can shop for all of your favorite products without breaking the bank.

Deliveries are free over £30 but any underneath that threshold are subject to a 5% fee of the total delivery cost. This fee helps to offset the cost of processing, packaging and delivering the items. The 5% fee is applied to the total cost of the delivery and is not a flat fee.

If you have any further questions, you can contact us using our contact form. To access the contact form, simply use the contact page on our website. Fill out the form with your full name, email address, and a detailed message of your inquiry. Once you've submitted the form, we'll respond as soon as possible with an answer to your question.
